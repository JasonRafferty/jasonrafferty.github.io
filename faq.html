Your Questions Answered

Getting Started

How do I book a session?

Simply send a WhatsApp message and we will arrange a time that works for you. The first session is a relaxed introductory lesson so we can assess where your child is and agree on a plan going forward.

How many sessions does it take to see improvement?

It varies depending on the student and subject, but most students notice increased confidence and understanding within 3 to 5 sessions. Grade improvements typically become visible over 6 to 12 weeks of regular tutoring.

Do you offer a trial session?

Yes, the first session is treated as an introductory lesson. It is a chance to meet, identify gaps, and see if we are a good fit before committing to regular sessions.

Sessions & Teaching

How long is each session?

Sessions are one hour. The first 55 minutes are dedicated to teaching, with the final 5 minutes reserved for a brief update with parents on progress and what was covered.

Are sessions online or in person?

Both. Online sessions are £25/hr and can be held anywhere via Google Meet, Zoom or Teams. In-person sessions are £30/hr and are available in the Manchester area.

What subjects and levels do you teach?

I tutor Maths, Chemistry, Physics, Biology, English and Computer Science from Primary (KS1–KS2) through to GCSE and A-Level.

Do you set homework?

Yes, where appropriate. Short focused tasks between sessions help reinforce what was covered and build good study habits. Homework is tailored to the student's level and never excessive.

Payment & Cancellation

How do I make payment?

Payment is made by bank transfer after each session, or in advance if preferred. Details will be shared once sessions are booked.

What is your cancellation policy?

I ask for at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule at no charge. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may be charged at the full session rate.

Do you offer block booking discounts?

I do not currently offer block booking discounts, but sessions are priced competitively and there are no hidden fees or registration costs.
