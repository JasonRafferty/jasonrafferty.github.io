Study Tips

📅

Plan ahead

Start early and break revision into small daily sessions to avoid last minute stress.

❓

Past papers first

Practise real exam questions to understand the question style and how marks are awarded.

🗣️

Ask questions

Never move on if something is not clear. Ask me during or after lessons if you are stuck or have any burning questions.

🔁

Spaced repetition

Review topics regularly so key methods, facts and exam techniques stay fresh.
