Getting Started

You are welcome to check my availability page to see the times I currently have free. Once you have an idea of what may work best, simply send me a WhatsApp message and we can arrange a suitable time, either online or in person.

Every student is different, but many parents notice their child becoming more confident and secure with the topic within the first 3 to 5 sessions. Clear improvements in grades usually take longer and are most often seen after 6 to 12 weeks of regular tutoring, depending on the student's starting point, consistency and the subject being studied.

For online lessons, students need a laptop, tablet or computer, a stable internet connection, and somewhere quiet to work. I use Google Meet for all online sessions and will send a link around 30 minutes before the lesson starts. Any worksheets or resources needed can be shared digitally during the session.

Sessions & Teaching

Sessions are one hour long. The first 55 minutes are focused on teaching, practice and supporting the student with the areas they are finding difficult. The final 5 minutes are set aside for a brief update with parents on what was covered, how the student got on and any next steps.

Payment & Cancellation

Payment can be made by bank transfer after each session, or in advance if preferred. I will share the payment details once your sessions have been arranged.

I ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule a session, as this helps make sure the time can be offered to another student who may be waiting for a lesson. Cancellations or changes made with less than 24 hours' notice may be charged a £10 cancellation fee.
